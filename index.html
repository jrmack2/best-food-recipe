Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy, classic Italian pasta dish made easy.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, sauté pancetta in a pan until crispy. Add garlic if using.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in grated Parmesan.
  4. Drain the pasta and save a little pasta water.
  5. Add spaghetti to the pan with pancetta. Remove from heat.
  6. Stir in egg-cheese mixture quickly, using pasta water to loosen if needed.
  7. Season with pepper, serve hot, and top with extra cheese.
